Pool rules and format for the TKE Masters Pool.

How the Pool Works

The page below is driven by the live Google Sheet feed for your pool. Participant totals and trend lines update automatically from that source.

  • Each participant drafts 8 golfers before the tournament begins.
  • For each round, only the lowest 6 golfer scores count toward that participant's round total.
  • The 6 counting golfers can change from round to round.

Scoring, Cut Rules, and Winning

  • If a golfer is cut, disqualified, or withdraws, a 10-stroke penalty is applied for that golfer.
  • Missed-cut golfers can be represented with 82s in later rounds for tracking, but the pool logic is driven by participant totals and penalties.
  • Round 1 through Round 4 participant scores are added together, then penalties are applied to reach the adjusted total.
  • The leaderboard is ordered by the lowest adjusted total.
  • The Pool Trends chart shows cumulative strokes behind the leader after each round: R1, R2, R3, and R4.
  • The participant with the lowest final adjusted total wins the pool.
